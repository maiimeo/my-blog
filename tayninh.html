Welcome to Tay Ninh

Tay Ninh is a vibrant city located in the southeastern region of Vietnam, famous for its stunning landscapes, rich history, and diverse culture. It is a popular destination for both locals and tourists looking to experience the beauty of nature and culture in one place.

Exploring The Holy See Temple

Tay Ninh is home to the famous Cao Dai Holy See Temple. This stunning architectural marvel combines various religious influences and serves as the center of the Cao Dai religion. Visitors can marvel at the intricate details of the temple and attend the colorful daily ceremonies held here.

The Beauty of Ba Den Mountain

Ba Den Mountain is one of Tay Ninh's most iconic landmarks. Standing at nearly 1,000 meters tall, it offers visitors a breathtaking view of the surrounding landscape. You can hike to the top or take a cable car ride to enjoy the panoramic view of the lush green forests and valleys.
