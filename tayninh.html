About Tay Ninh

Tay Ninh is a province located in the southeastern region of Vietnam, famous for its beautiful landscapes and religious sites.

Top Attractions

Ba Den Mountain

Ba Den Mountain is the highest peak in the Southeast region, offering beautiful views and historical temples.

Cao Dai Temple

This unique temple is the center of Cao Dai religion, an interesting mix of different religious influences.
