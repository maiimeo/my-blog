Overview

Tay Ninh is a charming province in southern Vietnam, known for its stunning landscapes and vibrant culture. It's famous for the Cao Dai Temple and the Ba Den Mountain, offering a perfect mix of nature and spirituality.

Top Attractions

Cao Dai Temple

A magnificent temple representing the Cao Dai religion, with its stunning architecture and colorful design.

Ba Den Mountain

The highest mountain in southern Vietnam, offering breathtaking views and a spiritual vibe.

Culture & Traditions

Tay Ninh has a unique blend of local customs, with strong influences from Cao Dai beliefs, as well as traditional Vietnamese culture. The people of Tay Ninh are known for their warmth and hospitality.
