About Tay Ninh

Tay Ninh is a beautiful province located in the southern part of Vietnam. Known for its unique culture, stunning landscapes, and historical landmarks, Tay Ninh is a must-visit destination for travelers looking to explore the heart of Vietnam.

Top Attractions in Tay Ninh

Ba Den Mountain

Ba Den Mountain is the highest mountain in Southern Vietnam and a perfect spot for hiking and exploring the natural beauty of the region.

Cao Dai Temple

The Cao Dai Temple is a stunning example of a religious architecture that combines several different religions, including Buddhism, Christianity, and Confucianism.

Tran Tau Lake

This serene lake is a hidden gem in Tay Ninh, ideal for relaxing and enjoying the peaceful atmosphere.
