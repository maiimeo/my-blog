About Tay Ninh

Tay Ninh is a province located in the southeastern region of Vietnam, famous for its beautiful landscapes and religious sites.

Top Attractions

Ba Den Mountain is the highest peak in the Southeast region, offering beautiful views and historical temples.

Upcoming Events

  • Annual Tay Ninh Festival - March 2025
  • Ba Den Mountain Hiking - June 2025

Culture

Tay Ninh is known for its unique blend of local and religious cultures, including the Cao Dai religion.

Ba Den Mountain

The highest mountain in the region, a spiritual site, and a popular hiking destination.

Food

Tay Ninh is famous for its delicious cuisine, including local dishes like "bánh tráng" and "mắm bò hóc".

History

The province has a rich history, including its role in Vietnam's war history.
