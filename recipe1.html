Pistachio Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped pistachios
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar (for coating)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Mix in the vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the flour and salt, mixing until combined.
  5. Fold in the chopped pistachios.
  6. Scoop small portions of dough and roll them into balls. Place them on the baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and let them cool for 5 minutes.
  9. Roll the warm cookies in powdered sugar to coat, then let them cool completely on a wire rack.
  10. Serve and enjoy!

Tips

  • For extra flavor, try adding a teaspoon of almond extract.
  • You can substitute pistachios with other nuts, such as almonds or pecans.
